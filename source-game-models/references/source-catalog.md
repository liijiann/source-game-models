# Source Catalog and License Gate

Use this catalog for discovery, not as proof that a particular asset is reusable. Terms and individual listings change. Open the live asset page and record the exact license before downloading.

## Recommended Search Order

| Source | Best for | Typical access | What to verify |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Kenney](https://kenney.nl/assets) | Cohesive game-ready packs, props, environments | Usually direct download | License on the selected pack, included license file, available 3D format |
| [Quaternius](https://quaternius.com/) | Stylized low-poly packs, characters, creatures, environments | Usually direct download | License on the selected pack, attribution request, rig and animation contents |
| [Poly Haven](https://polyhaven.com/models) | PBR props and environment assets | Direct download | Asset license, geometry/texture size, whether optimization is needed for realtime use |
| [Poly Pizza](https://poly.pizza/) | Searchable low-poly props and characters | Asset-dependent | Exact CC license, original author, download format, attribution |
| [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/) | Community game assets and themed packs | Asset-dependent | Every listed license, commercial/derivative permission, attribution, source provenance |
| [Sketchfab](https://sketchfab.com/) | Broad catalog and niche subjects | Downloadable filter; login may be required | Download permission, exact license, author, format, texture and polygon data |
| [Mixamo](https://www.mixamo.com/) | Rigged characters and reusable animation clips | Adobe login required | Current Adobe terms, project-use permission, redistribution restrictions, rig compatibility |
| [Fab](https://www.fab.com/) | Free marketplace listings across many styles | Account or launcher may be required | Current price is free, listing license, modification rights, engine/file access |
| [itch.io](https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-3d) | Indie asset packs and coherent kits | Creator-dependent | Creator-supplied license, commercial use, modification and attribution terms |

Prefer no-login sources when art and technical fit are comparable. Do not ask the user for credentials. A source being listed here does not make all of its assets acceptable.

## Search Strategy

Build queries from four parts:

```text
<subject synonym> + <art style> + <technical need> + <format/source>
```

Examples:

```text
stylized delivery van low poly rigged glb
modular medieval market stall game ready gltf
site:poly.pizza robot character animated
site:quaternius.com fantasy village pack
site:sketchfab.com "downloadable" CC0 forklift
```

Search both the exact noun and adjacent terms. For example, search `tram`, `streetcar`, and `trolley`; or `market stall`, `vendor booth`, and `bazaar stand`. Search packs when the game needs several related objects because one author or kit usually produces better visual consistency.

## Hard License Gate

Accept only a free asset whose live page or bundled license clearly permits the intended project use and modification.

Usually acceptable after verification:

- CC0 or equivalent public-domain dedication;
- CC BY with complete attribution;
- a free marketplace or creator license that permits modification and distribution as part of the built game.

Reject:

- no license, unknown author, or a repost without the original page;
- CC BY-ND or any other no-derivatives term;
- CC BY-NC or other noncommercial-only terms when commercial-friendly use is required;
- editorial-only, personal-use-only, preview-only, or evaluation-only assets;
- ripped assets from games, films, viewer caches, private repositories, or model previews;
- listings whose download, modification, or in-game distribution rights cannot be confirmed;
- paid assets for this skill's free-only workflow, even when they are the best visual match.

Treat custom marketplace terms as custom terms; do not relabel them as Creative Commons. Check whether attribution, a license notice, share-alike handling, or restrictions on source-file redistribution apply. When terms conflict or remain ambiguous, reject the asset and explain why.

## Evidence to Capture

For each selected asset, record:

- title, creator, pack or collection, and original asset URL;
- exact license name/version and license or terms URL;
- access date and whether login was required;
- downloaded filenames and supplied license/readme files;
- attribution text, if required;
- modifications and where the derived model is used;
- relevant redistribution restrictions.

Do not treat this workflow as legal advice. Surface uncertainty instead of making legal claims beyond the published terms.
